Our Restaurent




Significant to its name, THIRTY8 is located on the 38th floor boasting a 360-degree city
view to complement the elegance of its dining areas, bar and lounge, along with lively
show kitchens which serve exquisite cuisines like Western and Chinese. The captivating
panoramic view, the dramatic sense of décor and the splendour
of its cuisine are what makes THIRTY8 the signature restaurant of the hotel. Whether to entertain business
associates or celebrate special occasions with family and friends, THIRTY8 is the place
to be.
-
In view of overwhelming patronage we have received for afternoon tea and dinner,
THIRTY8 may encounter full-house situations where seats will not be available for walk-
ins. To avoid disappointments and to help us manage all expectations, we strongly
recommend advanced reservations to be made for dine-ins at THIRTY8.
